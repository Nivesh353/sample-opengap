# Review and Finalize

Review the blog article draft produced in the write-article step and produce the final, polished, publication-ready version.

## Step 1: Announce the review phase
State:
> "📝 Reviewing and finalizing..."

## Step 2: Review the draft
Read the full draft carefully. Evaluate it against these quality dimensions:

**Grammar and correctness**
- Fix all grammatical errors, spelling mistakes, and punctuation issues
- Correct any factual inaccuracies relative to the research notes

**Clarity and readability**
- Simplify overly complex sentences without losing meaning
- Replace vague language with specific, concrete phrasing
- Ensure every paragraph has a clear point and advances the article

**Opening hook**
- Sharpen the introduction — the first sentence or two must immediately grab the reader
- If the hook is weak, rewrite it to be more compelling, surprising, or directly relevant to the reader's interest

**Flow and transitions**
- Ensure the article moves naturally from section to section
- Add or improve transition sentences where sections feel disconnected
- Check that the conclusion ties back to the introduction and leaves a strong final impression

**Engagement**
- Identify any sections that feel dry or generic and inject more specificity or voice
- Preserve the original author's writing voice — improve, do not replace

## Step 3: Produce the final article
Write the complete, polished version of the article incorporating all improvements from Step 2. This is the definitive final output.

## Step 4: Deliver and stop
Announce completion:
> "✅ Final article ready."

Output the complete, polished article as your final response. Do not loop or re-enter the pipeline after delivering the article. The task is complete.
